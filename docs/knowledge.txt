Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is a technique where a language model 
retrieves relevant information from a 
knowledge base before generating a response.

A vector database stores embeddings, which are numerical representations 
of text that allow similarity search.

Embeddings convert text into vectors so that similar meanings are close together in vector space.

Large Language Models (LLMs) are AI models trained on large text data to understand and 
generate language.

Agentic AI refers to systems where AI can make decisions, use tools, 
and perform multi-step reasoning tasks.
